About WebcamTest — Free Online Camera Testing Tools

Discover how WebcamTest helps you verify camera functionality, troubleshoot video issues, and optimize webcam quality across all your devices for free. Explore our suite of browser-based camera testing tools built to check resolution, FPS, video quality, and more — no downloads required.

What is WebcamTest?

WebcamTest provides a simple online camera test that allows you to check if your webcam is working properly. Since it's a browser webcam test, you don't have to download or install any third-party software. Moreover, even if it may seem too simple, WebcamTest will test your camera regardless of its type or the device and operating system you are using.

And to surprise you even more, this webcam test will display a lot of useful information about your camera (for example, its name, video resolution, frame rate, aspect ratio, auto focus capabilities, as well as device specifications). In addition, if the tester detects any problems with your webcam, you will receive tips on how to fix them.

How It Works

Browser Permission Request

When you click "Test my webcam", we request camera access through your browser's secure WebRTC API. No plugins or downloads required.

Camera Detection & Analysis

Our system detects all available cameras on your device and analyzes their technical specifications including resolution, frame rate, and capabilities.

Real-time Video Testing

We display your camera feed in real-time, allowing you to see exactly what others would see during video calls or recordings.

Quality Assessment

Our algorithm evaluates your camera's performance and provides a quality rating along with detailed technical information.
